# Mechanical Engineer

In this document you'll find all the things related to the mechanical engineer like the mobility system, components of the car, and others.

## The car

The design of the car was made on computer using Fusion, an 3D designer app. The designs of the car were made thinking on been compact. Why? Well because if the car is smaller, can avoid obstacules more easily. All the parts that make up the car (chassis, mechanics, axles, etc.) were designed by our trusted mechanical engineer Efrain Ortiz. All the parts designed were printed using PETG and PLA filaments with the Creality 3 v3 ke and Dremel 3d45 printers. The chassis of the car is an squeletical design to be quicker to print, modify or change anything in case of be needed. The measurements of the car are: lenght = 25cm, width = 11.5cm, tall = 9cm. The car is RWD drived by a trusty TT motor and a MG996r servo motor for steering. The brain of the car consists of the microcontroller MOTION PRO RP2350 which is a versatile 30 programmable pin board capable of more than what we need it for. The car counts with 4 wheels of 51mm diameter, 2 apds9960 color sensors on front for a wider detecting range, 2 tmf8821 sensors on the front to read distance, vl53l0x sensors for read distance on each side and a bno08x for orientation and acceleration. On the top just below the microcontroller is the battery, this it's a JUOVI power bank that have a capacity of 37W/h and can supply 35W continuos, with a capacity of 10,000mAh and includes dual USB Type-C ports and one USB-A port.

## How it's built?

On top is the microcontroller MOTION PRO RP2350 and below of it is the battery. Before the front part is ubicated the TT motor and the MG996r servo motor for steering, and on top of it's ubicated the bno08x for the orientation. On both sides the car counts with the vl53l0x sensor to read distance. On the front the car counts with 2 APDS9960 for color reading and 2 tmf8821 for distance reading.
